RULES
--> You will have to control your character using up, down, left, right arrow keys.
--> Each '33' symbol in the given grid is food that you have to eat in order to gain 20 points.
--> Avoid bumping into '-1' and '42' symbols as you are not allowed to move over those regions.
--> This game is under development, therefore any kind of error or flaw/glitch is expected.
--> Enjoy :-)
